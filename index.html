You have a by grid. You want to create a path of increasing numbers from the first to the last field that follows the arrows. To do that, starting with 1 you drag its arrow to the field you want to label 2 and so on. Drag with the right mouse button to draw reversed arrows. When dragging from an empty field, you create a hint for yourself. The blue numbers are hints from the game, the red numbers are definitely wrong.

Nice.

Sorry but this game requires scripting and an HTML5 capable browser.
